About Me

The first song I ever wrote was when I was 8. It was originally a poem, but I decided to add piano chords and a melody. I've been playing piano since I was 5, and singing since looong before then. Nobody in my family is a musician, but I grew up listening to artists like the Beatles because of my dad. A lot of my inspiration comes from the music I listen to, as well as what I've experienced in life so far. I love to tell stories through music. An album that I feel really inspires me is "Grace" by Jeff Buckley. His voice, the instrumentation, and lyricism is absolutely beautiful and raw. It makes me feel like I was there with him when he was writing the album, which is exactly how I strive to portray my own music.

The music I write now is STILL just me and my piano, and occaisonally guitar. I love to collab with my friends and play music. The best cover I've done with a friend is TV by Billie Eilish with my friend Syd. It's our go to song to play when we're together. I've written numerous songs with my other friends, but they feel like more just for fun. My goal is to write more and collaborate with more people and eventually begin producing.
